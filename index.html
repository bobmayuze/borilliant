About

Borilliant is a group of people who would like to share skills of properly using technologies with people who want to make some differences. It is both our dream and our core idea that sharing the beauty of technology with you.

Whether you're a student looking to know about more about technology, an owner of a firm looking to have a plan to improve the productivity, proficiency or effiency, or an artist looking to share your projects, Borilliant will always be with you!

So far to now, Borilliant is a free service for people who want to make a difference with technologies.
